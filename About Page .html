Goals and Objectives

  • Increase public awareness of e-waste issues.
  • Encourage community participation in recycling programs.
  • Advocate for better legislation regarding e-waste disposal.

Background Information

E-waste is one of the fastest-growing waste streams in the world. The improper disposal of electronic devices can lead to severe environmental and health issues. Our project seeks to address these concerns through education and advocacy.
